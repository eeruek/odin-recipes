Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Home

Description

This recipe is about a simple Grilled cheese sandwich with Toast and cheddar

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Toast
  • Butter
  • 1 slice of Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients.
  2. Preheat a nonstick Pan.
  3. Butter both Toast one side generously.
  4. Place one Toast butter side down in the Pan and add 1 slice of cheese.
  5. Place second Toast butter side up on Top.
  6. Cook one side until lightly browned and than flip over.
  7. Same on the other side until cheese is melted
